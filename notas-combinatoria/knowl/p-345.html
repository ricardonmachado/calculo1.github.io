item a) Como \(C\subset B\text{,}\) temos \(B\cap C = C\text{,}\) logo \(\#(B\cap C) = \#C\) e \(\#(B\setminus C) = \#B - \#C\text{.}\) Logo

\begin{equation*} \#B - \#C = \overbrace{\#(B\setminus C) = 3\times \#(B\cap C)}^{\text{por hipótese}} = 3\times\#C. \end{equation*}

Ou seja, \(\#B = 4\times\#C.\)
